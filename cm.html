Communication at the Workplace: A Comic Strip

Good Communication

Scenario: A restaurant manager and two waitstaff are preparing for a busy shift.

Dialogue:

  • Manager: "Team, we’ve got a rush coming in. Let’s work together and stay focused!"
  • Waiter 1: "Got it! I’ll handle tables 5 to 8."
  • Waiter 2: "I’ll take care of the drinks. Let me know if you need help!"

Visuals: The characters are smiling and appear motivated.

Poor Communication

Scenario: The same restaurant, but the team is struggling due to poor communication.

Dialogue:

  • Waiter 1: "It’s not my job to remind you!"
  • Waiter 2: "I didn’t know table 4 needed drinks! No one told me."
  • Manager: "Why aren’t the orders out yet? You’re too slow!"

Visuals: Characters appear frustrated, with furrowed brows and frowns.
